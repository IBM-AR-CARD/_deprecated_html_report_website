Abstract

For people who do business with different people, they receive many business cards everyday. However, it is not very easy to manage them, find the desired card, and extract useful information. Also, the typical business card is not attractive and unique enough to get other people's attention.
Our project is an android and web application service for people to view and share business cards using AI-powered interactive 3D AR avatar. Users scan a QR code on the business card for triggering the AR avatar using Android app . Then AR figure will announce itself using Watson Speech to Text and give a 10-second overview of the owner of the card.After that, the user (the one who scans the QR code) can choose what details they want to know about the owner of the card.Finally, the AR avatar will offer the user the option of choosing the card as the favorite card. The user can find the list of favorite cards on the favorite page. Meanwhile, the user can see the scan history on the history page.
For website service, user can update their card details, design their own 3D AR avatar and view scan history and list of favorite cards.

Key features

AR avatar

view and share business cards using AI-powered interactive 3D AR avatar

History and favorite

can view scan history and list of favorite cards

Update details

can update personal details directly using app and website

Watson Assitant and Watson Speech to Text

use Watson Speech to Text and offer the user the opton of listening to more detalied info (using Watson Assitant)
